Cinnamon Roll Sugar Cookie

Roll chilled sugar cookie dough into about a 12 inch x 5 inch oval. Spread 12 tablespoons of soft butter all over the cookie dough. Add 1.5 cups of light brown sugar and 1.5 cups of ground cinnamon on top of the dough. Roll the dough to look like a little log and then use a sharp knife to cut into slices. Place each slice onto your baking sheet and bake to perfection. Add any kind of vanilla frosting to the tops.
