Boursin Pasta

Cook 1 package of bowtie/penne pasta according to package directions. Reserve some cooking liquid. While the pasta is cooking, heat a large saute pan over medium heat. Add a tablespoon of olive oil and 4 cloves garlic. Cook the garlic for 30 seconds. Add 1.5 cups of frozen peas. Add 1.5 cups of chicken broth and Â¼ cups of wine to the pan. Bring the mixture up to a bubble. Stir in 1 package Boursin Garlic and Fine Herbs of Boursin cheese. Add the cooked pasta. Add 1/3 cups of sliced sundried tomatoes, 1 tablespoon fresh dill, 1 teaspoon lemon pepper seasoning, and a grind of fresh pepper. Add 1/4 cup of Parmesan and toss well. Top with 1/4 cup toasted pine nuts and another 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese.
