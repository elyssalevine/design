Vegetable Salsa Salad

In a pot over medium high heat, combine the 87 oz broth, 16 oz of salsa, 2 cups of chopped carrots, 2 cups of chopped celery and 1 cup of frozen mixed vegetables. Simmer together for 20 minutes, or until vegetables are tender.
