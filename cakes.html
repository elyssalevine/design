Funnel Cake

Beat 1 egg into a bowl. Mix in 2/3 cups of milk. Sift 1 and 1/3 cups of flour, 2 tablespoons of white sugar, ¼ teaspoons of salt, and ¾ teaspoons of baking powder; beat into egg mixture until smooth. Pour vegetable oil into a heavy pan until it is 1 inch deep. Heat to 375 degrees F. Pour batter through funnel into oil with a circular motion to form a spiral. Fry until lightly brown; turn over to brown the other side. Cook to golden brown, and remove to drain on paper towels. Sprinkle with ¼ cups of confectioner's sugar while still warm.
