Lemon Chicken

Slice 2 boneless, skinless, chicken breast halves thinly. Season lightly with salt. In a medium non-stick frying pan, melt 1 tablespoon butter over medium heat. In a shallow bowl, season 1 cup of flour with some salt. Dredge the chicken in flour. Add a few dashes of olive oil to the melted butter. Place chicken in pan and cook for 2 minutes per side. Do this in a couple of batches, adding a bit of butter and a dash of oil each time. Remove cooked chicken to a warm platter. Add lemon juice to the pan and deglaze. Move off the heat if it’s burning. Add ¼ cup of the broth and bring pan back to the heat, stirring, until slightly thickened. Turn off heat and stir in 1 tablespoon butter. Dip chicken pieces into pan sauce, and return to warm platter. Pour remaining sauce over chicken. Grind a little black pepper over top. Garnish with parsley and a slice of lemon.
