Skinny Taco Dip

In a large bowl combine 8 oz 1/3 less fat Philadelphia cream cheese, 8 oz reduced fat sour cream, 16 oz jar mild salsa and 1/2 packet taco seasoning and mix well with an electric mixer. Spread on the bottom of a large shallow glass dish. Top with 2 cups iceberg shredded lettuce, 2 large diced tomatoes, 1 cup reduced fat shredded chedder cheese and 2.25 oz of sliced black olives. Serve with baked tortilla chips.
