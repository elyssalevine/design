Festive Fruit Punch

In a pitcher combine 4 cups of tonic water, 3 cups of ice cubes, 2 cups of pineapple juice, chilled, 2 cups of orange juice, 2 cups light cranberry juice, 1 sliced lime, 1 sliced orange, and 1 sliced carambola. Serve immediately.
